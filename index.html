Good to Know

⛽
Fill up in Salina, KS. Kansas gas is ~$3.90/gal. Salina is the natural mid-state stop — top off before Missouri prices kick in.
⏰
6:30am Friday departure is key. Denver rush hour runs 7–9am. Leaving at 6:30 clears the metro before traffic builds.
🏕
Book both campsites ASAP. mostateparks.com for Knob Noster · tnstateparks.com for Meeman-Shelby. Weekend tent sites fill fast.
🚿
Meeman-Shelby heads-up. A recent review flagged an active bathhouse renovation. Call (888) 867-2757 to verify before booking.
🎸
Memphis timing is ideal. Arriving early afternoon Saturday means Beale Street is lively but not yet packed. Central BBQ opens at 11am.
🏠
Day 3 arrives early (~1:30pm). Chattanooga route saves 35+ miles vs. Asheville. Home Sunday afternoon with the evening free.
📱
Download offline maps for Kansas. Cell coverage gets spotty in western KS. Download the I-70 corridor in Google Maps before leaving Denver.
🌤
I-24 through Tennessee is the payoff. The Highland Rim between Nashville and Chattanooga has rolling plateau views — a great final stretch home.
